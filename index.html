Who am I?

My name is Daniel Johansson. I am a 34 year old male born and raised in the costal town Falkenberg on the west cost of Sweden. I still live here and love small town feel and the proximity to the sea. For the last ten years I have been working at the customer servie of Hallandstrafiken, which I have been very pleased with, but currently I am studiyng at Campus Varberg to become a software developer.

My interests

Since starting my studies to become a Software Developer I have a newfound interest in coding. In fact, before I started my education/training I wasn't actually sure I would enjoy sitting infront of a computer writing lines of code.

Except from my aspiration of becoming a software developer I have set a goal that I will learn how to sail and acquire a kustskepparintyg (Costal yachtmaster Diploma). Up until today I have taken a one week course in sailing so I have a long way to go.

I also have a hobby of collecting seeds from various fruits and plants, making them sprout and trying to grow them in my small department.
